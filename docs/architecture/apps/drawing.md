# Drawing app architecture

> Idea stage

The Drawing app provides a creative canvas for users to draw, sketch, and create digital artwork with AI-powered assistance and feedback.

## Focus modes

### Feedback

Provides users with constructive feedback on their drawings to help them improve their artistic skills.

**Features:**
- Analyzes drawing technique, composition, proportions, and style
- Offers specific, actionable suggestions for improvement
- Identifies strengths and areas for development
- Provides guidance on:
  - Composition and layout
  - Line quality and control
  - Shading and values
  - Color theory and palette choices
  - Perspective and proportions
  - Anatomy and form
  - Style development
- Adapts feedback based on user's skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
- Can provide progressive feedback throughout the drawing process
- Suggests exercises and techniques to practice specific skills

**Use cases:**
- "Give me feedback on this sketch"
- "How can I improve the proportions in this figure drawing?"
- "What's working well in this composition and what needs work?"
- "Help me understand how to add better depth to my drawings"

### Other potential focus modes

- **Tutorial**: Step-by-step guidance for drawing specific subjects or learning techniques
- **Practice**: Structured exercises and challenges to develop specific skills
- **Style Exploration**: Help users experiment with different artistic styles
- **Reference Analysis**: Study and learn from reference images and master works
