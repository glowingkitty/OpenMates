# Revenue Model

## Key Questions to Answer

### Revenue Streams
- What are our primary revenue streams and how do they work?
  > **Suggested Answer**: **Credit-Based Usage (60%)**: Pay-per-use model for premium AI features; **Enterprise Subscriptions (25%)**: Monthly/annual subscriptions for business users; **Self-Hosting Support (10%)**: Infrastructure and support for self-hosted deployments; **Consulting & Training (5%)**: Custom development and AI education services.

- How do we balance monetization with open source values?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Transparent pricing, community revenue sharing, contributor recognition programs, and sustainable business model that benefits the community while maintaining open source values.

- What percentage of revenue comes from each stream?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Credit-based usage (60%), enterprise subscriptions (25%), self-hosting support (10%), consulting & training (5%), with plans to diversify revenue streams over time.

### Credit-Based System
- How does our credit-based system work for users?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Free tier: 100 credits/month for basic AI interactions; Premium tiers: $10/month for 1,000 credits, $50/month for 10,000 credits; Enterprise: Custom pricing based on usage and requirements; Transparent pricing with no hidden fees or usage limits.

- What features are free vs. paid, and how do we decide?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Basic AI interactions are free, premium features require credits, enterprise features require subscriptions, with decisions based on community input, user demand, and sustainability goals.

- How do we ensure the system is fair and transparent?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Transparent pricing, no hidden fees, community input on pricing decisions, regular pricing reviews, and clear communication about costs and benefits.

### Enterprise & B2B Revenue
- What enterprise features and services do we offer?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Advanced analytics and reporting, custom app development and integration, priority support and SLA agreements, on-premise deployment and management, and team collaboration and user management tools.

- How do we price enterprise solutions competitively?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Competitive pricing with closed-source alternatives, volume discounts for enterprise customers, transparent pricing, and value-based pricing that reflects the benefits of open source solutions.

- What support and consulting services do we provide?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Custom app development, enterprise support, training and onboarding, consulting services, and SLA and support agreements for business customers.

### Community & Open Source Revenue
- How do we generate revenue while maintaining open source values?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Contributor rewards program with revenue sharing, community-driven feature development funding, open source education and certification programs, conference and event sponsorship opportunities, and merchandise and community merchandise sales.

- What community-driven revenue models do we use?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Contributor rewards program, community-driven feature development funding, open source education programs, conference sponsorship, and community merchandise sales.

- How do we ensure community contributors are fairly compensated?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Contributor rewards program, revenue sharing with contributors, recognition programs, and transparent compensation and equity sharing.

### Pricing Strategy
- How do we price our services competitively?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Transparent, usage-based pricing with no vendor lock-in, competitive with closed-source alternatives, volume discounts for enterprise customers, and community pricing for non-profits and educational institutions.

- What pricing models work best for different user segments?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Credit-based pricing for individual users, subscription pricing for enterprise customers, volume discounts for large organizations, and community pricing for non-profits and educational institutions.

- How do we ensure pricing is transparent and fair?
  > **Suggested Answer**: Transparent pricing, no hidden fees, community input on pricing decisions, regular pricing reviews, and clear communication about costs and benefits.
