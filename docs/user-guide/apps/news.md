# News

> Search for current news articles on any topic.

## What It Does

The News app searches for recent news articles and headlines. It is optimised for finding timely, relevant news content and automatically filters out low-quality tabloid sources by default.

**Available skills:**

- **Search** -- Finds news articles matching your query. Results show article titles, descriptions, publication dates, and source information. You can search for multiple topics at once (up to 5 in parallel).

**Search options your mate can use:**

- Filter by freshness: past 24 hours, past week, past month, or past year (defaults to past week)
- Search in specific countries or languages
- Adjust safe search level
- Filter tabloid sources on or off

## How to Use It

- Ask about current events: "What is happening with the EU elections?"
- Search for industry news: "Latest AI news this week"
- Get specific updates: "News about SpaceX Starship launch"
- Your mate automatically uses the News app when you ask about current events or recent happenings.

## Tips

- Results appear as preview cards with article titles and descriptions. Click any result to read the full article.
- For more in-depth reading, ask your mate to read the full article using the Web app's read feature.
- Tabloid and low-quality sources are filtered out by default. If you specifically want those sources, just ask.
- News search defaults to the past week. Ask for "news today" or "news this month" to change the time window.
