# Videos

> Search for videos and get YouTube transcripts for summarising and analysis.

## What It Does

The Videos app helps you find videos on any topic and extract the text content from YouTube videos so your mate can work with it.

**Available skills:**

- **Search** -- Searches the web for videos, documentaries, tutorials, and clips on any topic. Results show video thumbnails, titles, and durations.
- **Get Transcript** -- Extracts the full transcript (subtitles) from a YouTube video. Supports multiple languages including English, German, Spanish, and French. You can get transcripts from several videos at once.

**Focus mode:**

- **Analyse Video** -- When you share a YouTube link, this mode gets the transcript and then provides a detailed summary, bias analysis, and fact-checks the key claims using web searches. It rates the overall reliability of the information and identifies what context might be missing.

## How to Use It

- Search for videos: "Find tutorials about watercolour painting"
- Get a transcript: "Get the transcript of this YouTube video: [link]"
- Summarise a video: "Summarise this video for me: [link]"
- Fact-check a video: Share a link and ask "Analyse this video for bias and accuracy"

## Settings and Memories

- **To Watch List** -- Save videos you want to watch later, with the video name, link, and channel.

## Tips

- Video search results appear as preview cards with thumbnails. Click to watch in your browser.
- Transcripts are useful for getting key points from long videos without watching the whole thing.
- The analyse mode fact-checks claims by running web searches, so you get an independent assessment of the video's accuracy.
- If YouTube subtitles are not available for a video, your mate will let you know.
