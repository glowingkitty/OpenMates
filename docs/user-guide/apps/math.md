# Math

> Accurate calculations, equation solving, and function plots.

## What It Does

The Math app gives your mate the ability to perform precise mathematical calculations. Instead of estimating answers, your mate sends the expression to a dedicated calculation engine that guarantees exact results.

**Available skills:**

- **Calculate** -- Evaluates mathematical expressions with guaranteed precision. Covers everything from basic arithmetic to advanced symbolic mathematics.

**What it can do:**

- Basic arithmetic: addition, subtraction, multiplication, division
- Algebra: simplify expressions, solve equations
- Calculus: derivatives, integrals
- Trigonometry and logarithms
- Unit conversions (kilograms to pounds, celsius to fahrenheit, etc.)
- High-precision calculations (up to 100 significant digits)

**Function plots:**

- When your mate describes a mathematical function, it can generate an interactive plot that appears as a preview card in the chat. Click to open the plot in fullscreen.

## How to Use It

- Calculate: "What is (4 times 22 times 7) divided by 2?"
- Solve an equation: "Solve x squared minus 4x plus 3 equals zero"
- Convert units: "Convert 100 kilograms to pounds"
- Differentiate: "What is the derivative of sin(x) times e to the x?"
- Plot a function: "Plot the function y equals x squared from -5 to 5"

## Tips

- Your mate automatically uses this skill whenever you ask a maths question, so you always get precise answers rather than estimates.
- Results show the expression that was evaluated and the exact result.
- For very high precision needs, you can ask for more decimal places (up to 100 significant digits).
- Function plots are interactive -- you can zoom and explore them in fullscreen.
