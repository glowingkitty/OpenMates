# Nutrition

> Search REWE Online for recipes by dietary tags, ingredients, and meal type.

## What It Does

The Nutrition app finds recipes from the REWE recipe database (~10,000 German-language recipes) and returns full details including ingredients, instructions, nutrition info, ratings, and dietary tags.

**Available skills:**

- **Search Recipes** -- Search REWE Online for recipes by dietary and category filters. Returns full recipe details: ingredients, step-by-step instructions, nutrition info, ratings, and health scores.

**Supported filters:**

- **Diet** -- fleisch, fisch, vegetarisch, vegan
- **Main ingredient** -- nudeln/pasta, kartoffeln, reis, gemuese, kuerbis
- **Effort level** -- einfach (easy), mittel (medium), schwer (hard)
- **Meal type** -- vorspeise, hauptspeise, dessert, beilagen, fruehstueck, suppen, auflauf, snacks, getraenke
- **Dietary form** -- laktosefrei, low-carb, glutenfrei, paleo, wenig-zucker, clean-eating
- **Baking** -- kuchen, torten, brot, muffins, cupcakes, plaetzchen
- **Occasions** -- fruehling, grillen, picknick, kindergerichte, geburtstag, guenstig, party

## How to Use It

- Find a quick weeknight dinner: "Show me easy vegetarian pasta recipes"
- Filter by diet: "Find vegan gluten-free desserts"
- Plan a meal: "Suggest a low-carb main course with chicken"
- Browse by occasion: "Recipes for a kids birthday party"

## Tips

- Recipes are sourced from REWE.de and are in German -- your mate can translate ingredients and instructions on request.
- Combine multiple filters to narrow results (e.g. `vegetarisch` + `glutenfrei` + `hauptspeise`).
- Each recipe card shows ingredients, prep time, servings, nutrition, and ratings. Click to see the full recipe in fullscreen.
- Saved recipes and meal plans are stored in your Nutrition memories so your mate can refer back to them later.
