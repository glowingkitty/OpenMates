# Docs

> Create formatted documents, reports, letters, and articles.

## What It Does

The Docs app lets your digital team mates create rich, formatted documents directly in the chat. When you ask for a report, letter, blog post, essay, cover letter, proposal, or any substantial piece of writing, your mate generates a document that appears as an interactive preview card.

**How documents work:**

- Documents are generated as formatted content with headings, paragraphs, lists, tables, and other standard formatting.
- Each document appears as a preview card in the chat. Click it to open the full document in a reader view.
- You can download documents as Word (.docx) files or copy the content to your clipboard.

## How to Use It

- Ask for any type of document: "Write me a cover letter for this job posting"
- Request reports: "Create a summary report of the key points from our discussion"
- Generate articles: "Write a blog post about sustainable gardening"
- Create formal documents: "Draft a rental agreement for a one-bedroom apartment"

## Memories

- **Writing Styles** -- Save your preferred writing styles (for example, formal business writing or casual blog style) so your mate can match your voice.

## Tips

- Your mate automatically chooses to create a document when the content is substantial and would benefit from proper formatting.
- Documents support headings, lists, tables, blockquotes, and other standard formatting elements.
- You can ask your mate to revise or modify a document after it is generated.
- For spreadsheet data, your mate will use the Sheets app instead.
