# Reminder

> Schedule one-time or repeating reminders that notify you at the right time.

## What It Does

The Reminder app lets you schedule reminders that can either nudge you back to a conversation or trigger your mate to perform a task at a scheduled time.

**Available skills:**

- **Set Reminder** -- Create a reminder for a specific time or a random time within a window. Supports one-time and repeating schedules (daily, weekly, monthly, or custom intervals).
- **List Reminders** -- View your upcoming reminders.
- **Cancel Reminder** -- Cancel a reminder you no longer need.

**Two types of reminders:**

- **Simple (notification only)** -- Your mate just sends you a notification. Use this when you want to be nudged back to something, like "remind me about this chat tomorrow." No response is generated -- you just get a ping.
- **Full (task trigger)** -- Your mate actually performs a task when the reminder fires. Use this when you want something done automatically, like "search the news every morning and summarise the headlines."

**Reminder targets:**

- **New chat** -- The reminder creates a fresh conversation when it fires.
- **Current chat** -- The reminder sends a follow-up message in the chat where you set it.

## How to Use It

- Simple reminder: "Remind me about this conversation tomorrow at 3pm"
- Scheduled task: "Every Monday morning, search for the latest tech news and give me a summary"
- Random timing: "Remind me sometime this week between 10am and 2pm to take a break"
- Repeating reminder: "Remind me every day at 9am to check my to-do list"

## Tips

- Set up email notifications in your account settings to receive reminders even when you are not using the app.
- Your mate uses your timezone automatically based on your account settings.
- Repeating reminders can have an end date or a maximum number of occurrences.
- You can cancel any pending reminder by asking your mate or through the list of active reminders.
