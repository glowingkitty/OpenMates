# Travel

> Search for flights, hotels, and track past flight details.

## What It Does

The Travel app helps you plan trips by searching for transport connections and accommodation, and lets you look up details of past flights.

**Available skills:**

- **Search Connections** -- Find flights for specific dates. Supports one-way, round trip, and multi-stop journeys. Results include airlines, departure and arrival times, number of stops, duration, and prices. You can sort by price, duration, departure time, or number of stops.
- **Search Stays** -- Find hotels, hostels, and other accommodation at any destination. Filter by price, star rating, check-in/check-out dates, and number of guests.
- **Get Flight** -- Look up the actual details of a past flight by its flight number and date. Shows the real flight path on a map, actual departure and arrival times, runway information, and any diversions. Useful for checking how a specific flight went.

**Search options for flights:**

- Travel class: economy, premium economy, business, or first
- Number of passengers
- Direct flights only option
- Preferred currency for prices

## How to Use It

- Search flights: "Find flights from Berlin to London on April 15"
- Round trip: "Search for round-trip flights from Munich to Barcelona, departing March 20 and returning March 27"
- Find hotels: "Find hotels in Paris for March 15-18, 2 adults"
- Look up a flight: "Show me the details of flight LH2472 on March 5"
- Compare options: "Search for the cheapest flights from Hamburg to Rome next weekend"

## Settings and Memories

- **Trips** -- Save your upcoming and past trips with destinations, dates, and notes.
- **Preferred Airlines** -- Record your favourite airlines for personalised recommendations.
- **Preferred Transport Methods** -- Save how you prefer to travel (train, plane, bike, etc.).
- **Preferred Activities** -- List activities you enjoy at destinations (beaches, museums, hiking, local food) for personalised travel suggestions.

## Tips

- Flight search results show prices and can be sorted to find the cheapest, fastest, or most convenient option.
- Hotel results include ratings, review counts, and nightly prices.
- The flight tracker works with completed flights only, not live tracking.
- Save your travel preferences to get more personalised search results and recommendations.
