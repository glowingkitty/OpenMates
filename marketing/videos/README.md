OpenMates video strategy

Rules:
- short videos (max 2-3 min)
- Authentic
- Come to the point
- Always recommend next video at the end of a video

Videos:

1. “Introducing OpenMates: digital team mates for everyday tasks & learnings. User interests first.”:
    1. Excited about potential of digital team mates for useful everyday tasks and learning, but frustrated with existing chatbots which are all bad, so started building better alternative
    2. Showing focus mode, app skills, settings and memories in action
    3. Easy to use, and control if you prefer
    4. Briefly talking about privacy & encryption 
2. “No ads, no training on your data, no enshittification” -> The ones who pay the bills, are the ones deciding the path of a project. With openmates, no advertisers or vcs pay the bills, but you, via pay per use. Meaning openmates is optimized for you and not for advertisers or vcs.
3. “How your mates use Apps to fulfill your tasks” -> introducing App Store, skills, focus modes, settings and memories
4. “Privacy by default. Because your data belong to you and no one else.“  -> chats & other sensitive data encrypted so only user device can decrypt them, auto detection and replacement of sensitive data, settings & memories data minimization. No training on data for ai models. “We don’t build any models, we make the models that already exist way more use useful.”
5. “All the latest AI models, without the headache.” -> auto selection so user doesn’t have to think about which model is the best for the task, but transparency about model & server provider + full control to overwrite the model.
