# Work / Life Balance in the age of AI agents

This meetup is a discussion group & exchange for everyone who is heavily using AI tools already and worries about burnout and work / life balance. But also for everyone else who is interested in discussing strategies for work / life balance in the age of AI agents.

We life in a time with acess to unprecedented AI powered tools for building software, hardware and other projects at a speed that was unthinkable just a few years ago. Projects that used to take weeks or even months and a full team can now be done in a few days by a single person.

But the heavy daily use of multitasking across multiple AI agents via Claude Code, Codex, OpenClaw and similar can not only be incredibly exciting and empowering, but also addictive. And the excitement about "Oh my god, I (feel like I) can build everything now!" can especially for people with ADD quickly lead to overworking and a lack of work / life balance.

Which opens the question: How does mental health and work / life balance look like, in the age of AI agents? And in the months and years to come?
